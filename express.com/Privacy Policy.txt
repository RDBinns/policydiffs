<p>For example, we might collect your name and street address if you register on our site.

<p>We may collect optional information like your gender and age.

<p>We may collect information you have provided in a resume or similar employment-related material.</p> We collect other information.
<p>If you use our website, we may collect your IP address and information about the browser you’re using.

<p>For example, if you use the E-mail-a-Friend or other similar features on our site, we collect your name and email address as well as that of your friend or family member.
