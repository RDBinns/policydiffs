 Privacy Statement CVS Caremark's Privacy Commitments <p>Effective Date: November 30, 2012.</p>
<p>At CVS Caremark, we are committed to ensuring your privacy.
This commitment guides our decisions about how, where, and when to collect and share your personally identifiable information.
Our goal is to provide you with clear, easy-to-read explanations about our information practices in one place.
This explanatory information applies throughout our businesses and across our websites, mobile apps and social media presences for cvs.com, caremark.com, cvscaremark.com and minuteclinic.com ("Our Sites").
We welcome your questions and feedback about our Privacy Policy or our privacy commitments, and invite you to Contact Us.
</p>
<p>As part of our privacy commitment to you, we want to share with you the basic components of our Privacy Policy:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Transparency.</strong> We will make our privacy commitments easy to find and easy to read.</li>
<li>
<strong>Notice.</strong> We will notify you of changes to our Privacy Policy before they take effect.</li>
<li>
<strong>Choice.</strong> You have choices about what information you provide to CVS and how CVS Caremark will use your information for marketing purposes.</li>
<li>
<strong>Security.</strong> We will use state of the art security safeguards to protect your privacy and the data entrusted to us.</li>
<li>
<strong>Control.</strong> We will not sell your personal information to anyone, for any purpose.
And when we share information with our subsidiaries or trusted service providers, they too must comply with our privacy commitments.</li>
<li>
<strong>Accessibility.</strong> We will listen to your feedback and answer your questions about our Privacy Policy, commitments, and practices.</li>
</ul>
<p>We recognize the trust you place in us to safeguard your privacy.
Our privacy commitments are and will remain a fundamental part of our business, and will apply to everyone who has a relationship with CVS Caremark, including patients, customers, and visitors to Our Sites.
These commitments may well grow as our business grows and we develop new ways to interact with you, but rest assured that we will keep you informed about these changes through our updated policies.</p>

</p>
<p>By using Our Sites, you signify your consent to our collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

<p>
<i>The Information We Collect About Customers and Users</i>
</p>
<p>CVS Caremark may collect different types of personal or other information when you visit Our Sites, when you place orders with us, and in other circumstances described in this Privacy Policy.
Some examples of the information we collect include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Contact Information, which we use for contacting you regarding your orders or for services which may be of interest to you;</li>
<li>Billing Information necessary for your financial relationship with us, including payment method data you store with us for the completion of orders;</li>
<li>Personal Health Information necessary for filling prescription orders from CVS Caremark.
We may also collect Personal Health Information while providing you services at CVS MinuteClinic.
This information is also protected under our Notice of Privacy Practices;</li>
<li>Technical Information relating to your use of our Sites or mobile apps.</li>
</ul>
<p>
<i>How We Collect Information About Customers and Users</i>
</p>
<p>CVS Caremark collects information in four primary ways:</p>
<ul>
<li>When you provide us with information when you purchase or interact with us about products or services offered by CVS Caremark;</li>
<li>When you register as a user of a CVS Caremark Site;</li>
<li>When we collect technical information automatically when you visit Our Sites.
and</li>
<li>When we obtain information about you from your employer or health care plan in connection with your prescription orders.</li>
</ul>

</p>
<p>CVS Caremark may use the information we collect in a variety of ways to enhance our customers' experiences, including to: </p>
<ul>

<li>Improve customer services;</li>
<li>Respond to your questions;</li>
<li>Deliver you customized content, advertising and savings that may be of interest to you;</li>
<li>Communicate with you regarding updates, offers, and promotions;</li>
<li>Monitor and ensure Site integrity and security;</li>
<li>Investigate, prevent, or take action relating to illegal activities, violations of our Terms of Service or Privacy Policy.

</p>
<ul>

<i>How We Share Personal Information</i>
</p>
<p>CVS Caremark will only share your personal information in manners described within this Privacy Policy.</p>
<ul>
<li>We do not sell your personal information to anyone for marketing purposes.</li>
<li>With CVS Caremark Companies: CVS Caremark and its subsidiaries may share your personally identifiable information with one another to ensure that your experiences as a customer or user of CVS Caremark Sites is as seamless and enjoyable as possible.</li>
<li>With Third Party Companies: In limited circumstances, CVS Caremark may share your personally identifiable information with third party companies to provide you with technologies, services, or content that may be of interest to you, or to assist us in fulfilling your orders.
In all instances in which CVS Caremark shares your personally identifiable information, we require that the recipient agrees to protect it in a manner consistent with this privacy policy.</li>
<li>In Other Circumstances: CVS Caremark may share your information in other limited circumstances, including:</li>
<ul>
<li>Responding to requests from law enforcement officials or judicial authorities or to otherwise cooperate with authorities pursuant to a legal matter;</li>
<li>Responding to matters of personal or public safety;</li>
<li>In litigation, investigations, and other legal matters where the data is pertinent;</li>
<li>To investigate security incidents;</li>
<li>In the event of the sale or transfer of CVS Caremark or of some of our assets, or in the context of similar business negotiations.</li>
</ul>
</ul>

</p>
<ul>

<p>
<i>Your California Privacy Rights</i>
</p>
<p>Under California Civil Code Section 1798.83, California customers are entitled to request information relating to whether a business has disclosed personal information to any third parties for the third parties' direct marketing purposes.
As explained elsewhere in this Privacy Policy, CVS Caremark will not sell or transfer your personal information with third party companies for their direct marketing purposes without your consent.
California customers who wish to request further information about our compliance with this statute or who have questions more generally about our Privacy Policy and our privacy commitments to our customers and Site users should not hesitate to Contact Us, or call us toll-free at (888) 607-4287.</p>
