<u>Browsing Information.
Cookies</u>
<br> When you visit the Site, our web servers may automatically recognize certain non-personally identifiable information about you, such as your domain name, access provider, IP address, and browser language.

We also may use cookies and/or a technology known as clear gifs to help us confirm your receipt of and response to our emails and to provide you a more personalized experience.
Please note that certain pages of the Site may not function properly if cookies are turned off.
Please consult your web browser’s Help documentation for more information about how to turn cookies on and off your browser.</p>
