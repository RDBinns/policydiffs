<p>Like many companies, we use "cookies" or other similar technology (including flash cookies, e-tags, cache browsers and GIFs etc) on the Website.

We use cookies to tell us, for example, whether you have visited the Website before or if you are a new visitor and to help us identify Website features in which you may have the greatest interest.

<p>When you visit this site, we automatically collect certain personal information, that we then aggregate so that it is not linked to a single, identifiable individual.

<p>We may also collect password information from you when you log in (if applicable), as well as computer and/or connection information.
