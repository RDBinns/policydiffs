<ul>
<li>registration-related information (such as name, addresses, e-mail addresses, telephone numbers, birth date, or gender);</li>

<li>other information specifically related to your use of Services, including information that you publicly post using tools made available by AOL.</li>
</ul>

<li>to fulfill your requests for products, programs, and services;</li>

<li>to conduct research about your use of AOL.
